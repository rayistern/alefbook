On all other nights we eat either leavened or unleavened bread - Matzah, but on this night exclusively Matzah.
שֶׁבְּכָל הַלֵּילוֹת אָנוּ אוֹכְלִין חָמֵץ אוֹ מַצָּה הַלַּיְלָה הַזֶּה כֻּלּוֹ מַצָּה:
Shebechol haleilot anu ochlin chameitz oh matzah, Halylah hazeh kulo Matzah
2
On all other nights we eat all kinds of vegetables, but on this night, bitter herbs.

Shebechol haleilot<br/>anu ochlin she-ar yerakot, Halaylah hazeh Maror
3
